Introduction
============

Welcome to `SweIoT <https://www.sweiot.com/>`_'s documentation. SweIoT is a leader in the development of versatile 
IoT sensors powered by advanced radar technology. Our sensors are designed for high performance 
in various environments, offering features like small form factor, IP67 rating for durability, 
and ultra-low power consumption. 

With millimeter-level accuracy, our sensors support real-time 
monitoring and predictive maintenance across multiple applications, including presence 
detection, motion sensing, and inventory management. Explore our documentation to learn more 
about our products and how to integrate them into your projects.

`Download PDF <https://sweiotdocs.readthedocs.io/en/latest/_static/Getting-Started-Guide-A121-XE126.pdf>`_
